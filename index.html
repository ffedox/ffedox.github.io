About me

Hi, I’m Alice! 👋🏻 I hold a Master's degree in Translation Technology and have completed both a Research Internship and a Fellowship at the University of Bologna, where I focused on Natural Language Processing (NLP), speech-to-text models, and fine-tuning Transformer-based models. I now work as a Language Technology Consultant, specializing in NLP and AI for text and speech data processing.

LinkedIn Profile

Python, NLP & AI Projects

I developed a system for automatic isotopy identification in medical dramas by expanding a dataset with subtitles and keyframes from Grey's Anatomy, using Transformer models like CLIP, BERT, and MMBT. I also created a semi-automatic pipeline for extracting a parallel corpus from English and Italian Wikipedia articles, along with a Selenium-based web scraper to extract articles and comments from Il Giornale, automating content retrieval for analysis.

Translation and Localization Projects

I have extensive experience translating, revising, and proofreading texts and multimedia content across specialized domains, including technical, commercial, and publishing sectors. This includes working with both written materials and audiovisual products, ensuring accuracy and consistency while tailoring content for specific audiences.

Publications and Projects

As a Language Technology Specialist and former Research Fellow at the University of Bologna, my work has focused on advancing tools and methodologies in linguistic analysis and multimedia content classification. During my fellowship, I developed innovative tools for corpus construction, managed large-scale datasets, and conducted experiments with speech-to-text models to improve transcription accuracy for linguistic research. Additionally, as a Research Intern, I contributed to a project investigating the success factors of medical dramas, comparing unimodal and multimodal classifiers trained on subtitles and/or images using models such as BERT and MMBT, to automatically identify narrative content in scenes. https://www.unibo.it/sitoweb/alice.fedotova2/contenuti-utili
